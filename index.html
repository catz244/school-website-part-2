Campus Life

Library

Established in 1955.

There are many books in the Library. Some of them are related to magic spells, magical creatures, dark magic and more. There is also a restricted section which a student needs a permission form from a professer.

Hall

Established in 1953.

This is where meetings, announcments and meals happen. There are four long tables one for each house. For light there are hundreds of candles floating in mid air.

Classrooms

Established in 1952.

This is where they have different classes like defence against the dark arts, transfiguration class, charms class, and more.

Achievements

Quidditch

In a quidditch match there are 2 teams. It's like football on a flying broom but instead of goal posts there are 3 loops on either side. There are seven members in a team - 1 Keeper- who guards the hoops to prevent the opposite team from scoring a goal, 2 Beaters- who prevent their team members from bludgers using a club(round iron balls that hit players during game), 3 Chasers- who score by passing the quaffle through the three hoops of their team, 1 Seeker- who looks for the tiny,fluttering golden snitch which gains 150 points to tge catcher's team. And the game ends when the Snitch is caught.

Triwizard Tournament

The Triwizard tournament is a competition in which 3 schools participate. Only one person from each school will be selected by a magial goblet called the goblet of fire. One need to score high marks in each of the 3 deadly and dangerous tasks in order to win.
